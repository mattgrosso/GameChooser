In this method you will begin by nominating games to be considered. Once you are done nominating, click the 'start process' button. Next you must take turns casting votes among the nominees. In the first round each person will cast votes worth 1pt. In the second round the votes are worth 2pts and in the third round the votes are worth 3pts. In-between voting rounds the totals will be displayed and after the third round is complete the game with the highest total points is the winner.
